the works of william morris

Course: Visual Communications

InDesign and Photoshop

This is an accordion book about the work and life of 19th century designer William Morris. It uses Morris' typeface he designed, as well as his textile and wallpaper designs. The designs were meticulously selected and edited digitally from existing archival files. This piece not only informs the reader about Morris' life, but attempts to display his art within the book's design. It is hand bound and cut.
